CSS

  • Selectors
    • star: * - Applies to everything
    • descendants: li a - Applies to all <a> anchor tags within <li> tag
    • adjacent: h4 + ul - Applies to any <ul> directly after any <h4>
    • attribute: a[href="http:www.google.com"] or input[type="checkbox"] - Applies to elements with certain attribute values
    • nth of type: ul:nth-of-type(3) - Applies to the nth of an element on a page (or in a list) (in this case, the 3rd <ul>; Note: For <li>, it does the nth <li> in every list; Note: can also use terms "even" and "odd" )
